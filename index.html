About Us

Maple County's Finest Roleplay (MCFRP) was rebranded from Concord State Roleplay on Febuary 26th, 2024 with the goal to provide one of the best roleplay experiences to the game of Maple County. MCFRP has a lot of things that set it apart from competitors, such as a premium CAD/MDT system for all members, TeamSpeak communications for departments, a rising DOJ system, housing system, custom liveries, the fact that the server is VC only, and more!
